  • To cancel your Netflix subscription, follow these steps: 1. Log in to your Netflix account on a browser or through the app. 2. Go to Account Settings: Click on your profile icon (top-right corner) and select "Account" from the dropdown menu. 3. Cancel Membership: Under the "Membership & Billing" section, you’ll see a "Cancel Membership" button. Click on it. 4. Confirm Cancellation: Follow the prompts to confirm your cancellation.
